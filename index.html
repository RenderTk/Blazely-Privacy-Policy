Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

Blazely is built with privacy first. We believe your data belongs to you. Almost everything stays on your device, and we only collect the minimum information necessary to provide you with a secure login experience.

Information We Collect

Authentication Information

When you create an account with Blazely, we collect and store:

  • Email address or username
  • Encrypted password
  • Account creation date

This information is necessary to authenticate your identity and provide you secure access to the app.

Local Device Storage

Everything else stays on your device. All your app data, preferences, content, and activity are stored locally on your device and are never transmitted to our servers. This includes:

  • All user-generated content
  • App settings and preferences
  • Usage data and activity
  • Any files or media you create or store in the app

What We Don't Collect

We Do NOT Collect:

  • Location data
  • Device information or identifiers
  • Usage analytics or tracking data
  • Cookies for advertising or tracking
  • Third-party analytics data
  • Any content you create within the app
  • Contacts, photos, or other personal files

How We Use Your Information

The limited authentication information we collect is used solely for:

  • Verifying your identity when you log in
  • Allowing you to reset your password if needed
  • Providing customer support when requested
  • Preventing unauthorized access to your account

Data Storage and Security

Your login credentials are stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your authentication information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Since all other app data is stored locally on your device, you maintain complete control over that information.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your information with third parties. Your login information is never provided to advertisers, data brokers, or any other external parties.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your account information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated login data
  • Update or correct your login credentials
  • Export any data we hold (limited to login information)

Data Retention

We retain your login information for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will permanently delete your login credentials within 30 days. All local data on your device can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date below.
